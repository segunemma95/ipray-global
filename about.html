About iPray Global

iPray Global is a movement created to unite believers in a 24-hour prayer chain, interceding for revival, nations, churches, and youth across the world.

Our mission is simple: gather, pray, and transform lives through the power of God.

Our Vision

  • Mobilize 1 million young people in prayer
  • Build a global prayer network
  • Raise intercessors for revival

About iPray Global

iPray Global is a worldwide prayer platform uniting believers across all nations. Our mission is to build a praying generation committed to kingdom advancement, soul winning, and spiritual growth through intercession.

Our Vision

To raise a community of believers praying daily for revival across the nations.

Our Core Assignment

  • Daily intercessory prayers
  • Kingdom advancement and soul winning
  • Spiritual edification and growth
  • Unity across nations through the Holy Spirit
